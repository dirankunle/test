An Efficiency Driven Customer Centric Company

Camouflage Towers Ltd is an indigenous telecommunications Infrastructure providing company incorporated in 2022 under the Federal Republic of Nigeria, geared towards providing Network expansion and site build turnkey services to the telecommunications industry. We are one of the fastest growing privately owned, telecommunication Infrastructure Company providing built to suite towers and co­-location service for the Mobile Network Operators and Internet Service Providers in Nigeria.

Nigeria remains our target and the biggest market to operate and deliver quality service to targeted customers. We have developed deep expertise in all aspects of telecoms infrastructure provision.

Our Parent company CSAL, have been and is still the certified and approved company since 2014 handling all telecommunications sites acquisitions on all military facilities across the country for all MNO’s network expansion activities including colocation companies as well. In order to perform more and seamlessly deliver on extraordinary efficiency and effectiveness on customer service delivery, Camouflage Towers was born.

We are partnering with leading Mobile Network Operators such as MTN, Airtel, 9mobile, Globacom etc. Camouflage Towers Ltd is not only serious about delivery on our promises, but it is imperative for us to impress our business partners and clients, as we solidify these long lasting and rewarding relationships.

We believe in relationships and nurturing thereof, are fundamental in business; combined with efficient service delivery, ensure our clients are clients for life by making sure that they get the best customer services they deserve.
